# Mutsumis_Music_Player
**Chinese version:**

本项目是我2025年春季学期MATLAB GUI课程（MATLAB基础及图形用户界面设计）实践作业的V1.0版本，指导老师是西北工业大学航海学院的郭志巍老师。

我的音乐播放器主题是日本动画《BanG Dream! It's MyGO!!!!!》和《Ave Mujica》。受室友的启发以及我个人的兴趣，我选择了动画中的角色若叶睦作为主要设计对象。音乐播放器支持从个人电脑导入音乐文件并进行操作（例如播放、暂停、恢复、停止、播放上一首和下一首）。

此外，我还实现了以下功能：

1. 音乐列表及其显示界面；
2. 音乐特效，包括音乐风格（例如爵士、摇滚、贝斯等）、播放速度、混响、高水准音乐效果（例如演唱会、现场场景、合唱等）以及人声减音；
3. 用户界面坐标轴上的时域波形；
4. 音乐播放进度滑块；
5. 音乐录制旋转及相应用户界面；
6. 实时音量调节；
7. 显示日期和正在播放的音乐名称；
8. 菜单控制和附加说明；
9. 额外惊喜选项。

项目最终成果（包括PC端App、MATLAB App、web端App）可以通过以下链接获取：[百度网盘](https://pan.baidu.com/s/1fTRcYeFnU4vbTBNYqXzDiw?pwd=aka6)。

祝您使用音乐播放器愉快！

享受音乐，感受少女乐队的快乐与美好，并在音乐播放器中发现惊喜！

**English version:**

This project is the V1.0 version for my pratical work of MATLAB GUI course in the spring semester of 2025, guided by the video of Zhiwei Guo (teacher for this course).

The topic for my music player is the Japanese animation *BanG Dream! It's MyGO!!!!!* and *Ave Mujica*. From the inspiration of my roommate and my personal interest, I choose the character Wakaba Mutsumi of the animation as my main designment. In the music player, you can import music files from your personal PC and operate on them (e.g. play, pause, resume, stop, play previous piece of music and play next piece of music). 

Addtionally, I implement following functions: 

1. Music list and its display interface;
2. Music effect, including music styles (e.g. jazz, rock, bass, etc.), playing speed, reverberation, high standard music effect (e.g. concert, live scene, chorus, etc.) and human voice cutting-down;
3. Time domain waveform in UI-axes;
4. Music playing process slider;
5. Music record rotivate and corresponding UI;
6. In-time volume adjustment;
7. Date display and the name of music playing display;
8. Menu control and additional notes;
9. Extra surprise options.

The final result of the project (comprehensive PC end App, MATLAB App, web end App) can be received as follows: [Baidu Netdisk](https://pan.baidu.com/s/1fTRcYeFnU4vbTBNYqXzDiw?pwd=aka6).

To enjoy music, to feel the joy and beauty in the girls' band, and to find surprise in the music player!
